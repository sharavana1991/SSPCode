About


Brief Description

World Time Buddy (WTB) is a web service that transforms, otherwise tricky, tasks like time zone conversion and international meeting or conference call planning into an intuitive, super-quick and painless experience.

Why yet another time site?

Most exisiting world time sites and meeting planners have that 90-ies feel and interaction models that frustrate users. It was time to apply modern tools and knowledge to this space.

If you haven't yet experienced it, we invite you to simply find a good time to call, say, Minsk, using some of the well-established converters and meeting planners. Then compare that experience to WTB.

Feedback

A good number of WTB features were created or changed for the better because of the feedback from our users. If you'd like to help, please let us know your ideas, bug reports, suggestions, frustrations and thoughts.

Data Sources

The industry-standard TZDATA is the primary source for time zone offsets and daylight saving rules. It is enriched with location names from the open GeoNames database.
